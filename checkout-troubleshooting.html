🛒 Checkout Troubleshooting Guide

Having trouble placing an order? This guide will help you resolve common checkout issues.

❌ Error: "Failed to place order: Resource not found"

Failed to place order: Resource not found

🔍 Possible Causes:

  • You're not logged in
  • Your email is not verified
  • Products in your cart no longer exist
  • Invalid product IDs in cart

✅ Solution Steps:

  1. Login First: Make sure you're logged in to your account
  2. Check Email: Verify your email if you haven't already
  3. Clear Cart: Remove all items and add them again
  4. Try Again: Attempt to place the order

❌ Error: "Not authorized, token failed"

Unable to start online payment: Not authorized, token failed

✅ Solution:

  1. Your session has expired
  2. Click "Logout" and login again
  3. Try placing the order again

❌ Error: "Please verify your email"

Please verify your email before placing orders

✅ Solution:

  1. Check your email inbox for verification link
  2. Click the verification link
  3. If no email found, contact support
  4. For testing: All users are now auto-verified
