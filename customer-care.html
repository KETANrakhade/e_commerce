Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
How do I exchange a product?
Click on "Exchange Product" above and fill in the form with your order details. You can exchange for a different size or color. Our team will arrange a pickup and deliver the exchanged item within 5-7 days.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (available in select cities) takes 1-2 business days. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery (COD). All payments are secure and encrypted.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel your order before it ships. Go to "My Orders" and click "Cancel Order". If the order has already shipped, you can return it after delivery.
How do I track my order?
You can track your order by clicking "Track Order" above or visiting the "My Orders" page. You'll also receive tracking updates via email and SMS.
